About

Upspire Studio was founded by Shawn Kemp, a designer and technologist with over two decades of experience building products and communities at the intersection of technology and purpose.

Prior work includes Creative Director roles at Microsoft Xbox, co-founding ActionSprout (serving 150,000+ nonprofit clients globally), and strategic advisory work with organizations focused on regenerative systems.

Today, Upspire Studio operates as a consulting practice and holding company, partnering with select clients on projects where craft, intention, and impact align.

How We Work
